Pizza

Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Sprinkle cheese generously on top.
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake in the oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
